Keeping My Memories Alive

My very recent newfound interest has been creating risograph animations using videos in my camera roll or old home videos. I get a lot of inspiration for my work from

  • Memories
  • Experiences
  • and Nostalgia

I like to feel like I’m keeping those memories alive by bringing them back to life in this new form.

What Is a Risograph?

A risograph is a stencil based printing machine that originated in Japan. It’s described as a digital screen printer that uses eco-friendly soy based ink that creates unique, vibrant colors. It’s also known for creating a slightly imperfect grainy texture from the misregistration, it’s part of the charm.
